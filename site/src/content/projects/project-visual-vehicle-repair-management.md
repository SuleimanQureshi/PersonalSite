## Plain English Summary

When a vehicle arrives for repair, documenting every dent, scratch, and panel damage manually is slow and inconsistent. This system uses cameras and machine learning to automatically photograph and catalog all visible damage, then feeds that information directly into the repair workflow — eliminating paperwork and creating an auditable digital record.

---

## Problem

Manual vehicle damage documentation is time-consuming, subjective, and creates inconsistent records that complicate repair planning, insurance claims, and quality assurance. A digitized, ML-driven approach can standardize and accelerate the intake process.

---

## Approach / Methodology

- Computer vision pipeline for vehicle damage detection and localization
- ML models for damage classification (dents, scratches, cracks, etc.)
