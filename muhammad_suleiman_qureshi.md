## Final Year Project

### Autonomous EV Tow Truck Control System

_Industry Collaboration: IMC Toyota_

- Retrofitted an electric tow truck with electronic sensing and actuation by designing custom PCBs, power electronics, and embedded control hardware.
- Performed system identification to obtain accurate vehicle dynamics models.
- Developed and tuned PID controllers for steering and velocity regulation.
- Implemented **Model Predictive Path Integral (MPPI)** control using ROS2 NAV2 for real-world navigation.
- Designed a hierarchical **PID-MPPI control architecture**: inner-loop PID for real-time steering/velocity regulation; outer-loop MPPI for trajectory planning and obstacle avoidance.
- Achieved steering accuracy of **±1°** and velocity error of **±0.3 m/s** under real-world constraints.
